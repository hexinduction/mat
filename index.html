We value safety as an extension of community care. When we talk about safety, we are talking about privacy, confidentiality, and respecting boundaries; we do not surveil, control, or criminalize our own community. We are also committed to using PPE and sanitation.

Please keep in mind, we may not be able to fulfill all requests, as our inventory is based on community donations. We always do what is possible through community support, however. Thank you for your patience and grace. We are guiding ourselves by ancestral knowledge and experiences.
